Fill your Monday nights with music and friends!
Join us this summer for our annual sing-alongs of beloved choral masterworks. You bring the voice; we supply the scores and the accompaniment; and a variety of local conductors stitch it all together.

Admission is $20 for each sing, or you can purchase a FLEXPass that gives six entries for $100. Use it for one entry to each of the six sings, or six entries to one sing, or any mixture. (Students under 25 get free admission with ID.)

For the safety of all participants, attendees will be required to comply with current masking requirements.
